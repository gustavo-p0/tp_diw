BANKSY

Revered by many, Banksy is an anonymous English artist who composes works full of social and political criticism. With a unique aesthetic, Banksy is considered by many to be the most famous graffiti artist in the world. His works can be admired in England, the United States, France and other parts of the world and are often the subject of controversy.

ARYZ

Aryz is a Spanish artist well known for producing his large-scale art on huge murals and building facades. Composing paintings that portray unreal figures, such as aliens, animals and strange people, this graffiti artist's work is known around the globe.

MÜLLER

Specialist in street art, Edgar Müller is a famous German graffiti artist who demonstrated his love for art since he was a child. With works that draw attention to their beauty, the technique presented by Edgar Müller is astonishing, as the artist produces three-dimensional drawings that hypnotize and promote optical illusion effects.

BASQUIAT

Blunt and critical, Jean-Michel Basquiat was an emblematic American artist who produced works full of social and political themes. Exploring both street art and exhibiting in the world's largest museums, Basquiat is considered a pop icon.

DAIM

Better known as Daim, German Mirko Reisser is one of the most famous graffiti artists in the world. Composing pieces falling out chin, Daim's work presents the illusion of 3D and high technical quality.

GÊMEOS

Considered the most famous graffiti artists in Brazil, Os Gêmeos, Otávio and Gustavo Pandolfo, have works scattered around around the world. Working with recyclable materials, these famous graffiti artists address themes varied in their art, producing both social content and those related to family themes.
